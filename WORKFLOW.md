# H3GameApp Match Day Workflow

Follow these steps to manage a soccer/football match from initial setup to final whistle.

## 1. Pre-Game Preparation
*   **Team Roster:** Ensure all players are added in the **Roster** tab. Include names, jersey numbers, and preferred positions.
*   **Squad Selection:** Go to the **Game Day** tab.
    *   Select the players available for today's match.
    *   Choose the game format (11v11, 9v9, or 7v7).
    *   Tap **Tactics Board** to proceed.

## 2. Tactical Setup
*   **Formation:** Select your desired formation from the chips at the top (e.g., 4-4-2, 4-3-3).
*   **Initial Lineup:** 
    *   Drag players from the **Bench** at the bottom and drop them into the empty slots on the field.
    *   The bench is divided into **READY** (fresh) and **RECOVERING** (previously played) sections.
*   **Clearance:** If you need to start over, tap the **Clear** button in the bench header.

## 3. Match Management (The Command Center)
Once the match begins, the **Tactics Board** becomes your primary hub.

*   **Kick-Off:** Tap the **Play** button in the header to start the Match Clock and Rotation Timer.
*   **Scorekeeping:**
    *   **Edit Names:** Tap the team names ("Home"/"Away") to enter actual opponent names.
    *   **Record Goals:** Tap the large score number to increment.
    *   **Mistake Fix:** Long-press the score number to decrement.
*   **Tracking Rotations:**
    *   Watch the **ROTATION** timer to see how long the current group has been on the field.
    *   The app will alert you based on your preferred interval (5 or 10 mins).

## 4. Performing Substitutions
*   **Visual Swap:** Drag a player from the **Bench** and drop them directly onto an **occupied** slot on the field.
*   **Target Highlight:** The player being replaced will glow indigo during the drag.
*   **Auto-Record:** Dropping the player automatically:
    1.  Swaps the players on the Tactics Board.
    2.  Resets the **ROTATION** timer to `00:00`.
    3.  Moves the "Recently Off" player to the **RECOVERING** section of the bench.
    4.  Updates the Match Log (Matrix) in the background.
*   **Planned Move:** Tap the **Refresh/Next** button in the header to manually advance to the next rotation block without a swap.

## 5. Reviewing the Match Log
*   Switch to the **Substitution** tab at any time to see analytics.
*   **Playtime Leaderboard:** See which players have the most minutes.
*   **Match Timeline:** View a visual "On/Off" history of the entire game.
*   **Summary:** View the current score and total elapsed match time.

## 6. Full Time
*   Tap **Pause** when the game ends.
*   Review the final Match Log for post-game analysis.
*   To start a new match, use the **New Game** action (accessible in the Substitution tab summary).
