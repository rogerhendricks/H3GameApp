# Standard 11v11 Soccer Positions

This document defines the standard soccer positions, abbreviations, and roles as used in the H3GameApp.

## 1. Goalkeeper
*   **Abbreviation:** GK
*   **Full Name:** Goalkeeper
*   **Role:** Shot-stopping, organizing defense, and initiating attacks.

## 2. Defenders
*   **Abbreviation: CB** | **Full Name:** Center-back
    *   **Role:** Defensive backbone, blocking shots, initiating play from deep.
*   **Abbreviation: RB** | **Full Name:** Right-back
    *   **Role:** Defending the right flank, overlapping runs, crossing.
*   **Abbreviation: LB** | **Full Name:** Left-back
    *   **Role:** Defending the left flank, overlapping runs, crossing.

## 3. Midfielders
*   **Abbreviation: CDM** | **Full Name:** Defensive Midfielder
    *   **Role:** Shielding defense, breaking up play, defensive pivot.
*   **Abbreviation: CM** | **Full Name:** Central Midfielder
    *   **Role:** Linking defense and attack, box-to-box play, dictating tempo.
*   **Abbreviation: CAM** | **Full Name:** Attacking Midfielder
    *   **Role:** Primary playmaker, assists, creating scoring opportunities.
*   **Abbreviation: RM** | **Full Name:** Right Midfielder
    *   **Role:** Wing play from midfield, crosses, defensive support on right.
*   **Abbreviation: LM** | **Full Name:** Left Midfielder
    *   **Role:** Wing play from midfield, crosses, defensive support on left.

## 4. Forwards
*   **Abbreviation: ST** | **Full Name:** Striker
    *   **Role:** Primary goal-scorer, holding up the ball, penalty area movements.
*   **Abbreviation: RW** | **Full Name:** Right Winger
    *   **Role:** Stretching defense, speed, dribbling, crossing from the right.
*   **Abbreviation: LW** | **Full Name:** Left Winger
    *   **Role:** Stretching defense, speed, dribbling, crossing from the left.
*   **Abbreviation: CF** | **Full Name:** Center Forward
    *   **Role:** Playing between CAM and ST, link-up play, creative attacking.

---

## Standardization for H3GameApp
The following abbreviations are used consistently across the **Team Roster** (selection) and **Tactics Board** (field labels):

**GK, RB, CB, LB, CDM, CM, CAM, RM, LM, RW, LW, ST, CF**
